Getting There

Trailheads are spaced along the corridor from Lexa in the north to Arkansas City in the south, with access points near Barton, Elaine, Mellwood, Watson, and Rohwer. Use the trail map above to find the closest starting point.

What to Bring

Water, sun protection, and a repair kit are essential — services are spread out between towns. A wide-tire or gravel bike is recommended for the crushed-stone surface.

Trail Etiquette

Keep to the right, pass on the left with a clear call-out, and yield to other trail users at road crossings. Pack out what you pack in.

Best Times to Visit

Spring and fall offer the mildest riding conditions in the Delta. Summer can be hot and humid — plan for an early start and extra water.
